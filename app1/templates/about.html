About Bali Bamboo Resort

About Us

Finding Joy in the Beauty of Nature

Discover the brand-new 3-star resort located at Devikulam, the coolest point in Munnar, nestled in the untouched region of this hill station where life moves at a slower pace, surrounded by tea gardens and forests. Enjoy a cozy and comfortable stay suitable for families and couples, situated near Meesapulimala, Kurishumala, and Chokramudi trekking trails. The Thief Cave is also nearby. Experience the cool climate where even in the noontime refreshing breezes sweep through the tea gardens.

Nestled in the cool climate of Munnar, our brand new 3-star hotel offers an idealistic location to experience all the wonders this enchanting destination has to offer. Whether you're a couple seeking romance or a family in search of adventure, our establishment caters to all, providing the perfect blend of climate, luxury, and affordability. With spacious rooms, a multi-cuisine restaurant, and a rejuvenating spa, our hotel ensures a comfortable stay without breaking the bank. Discover the magic of Munnar while enjoying the comforts of our hospitality—a truly irresistible choice for discerning travelers seeking a memorable and budget-friendly getaway.

Nearby Attractions in Wayanad 🏔️

Discover breathtaking natural wonders and ancient heritage sites just minutes away from our resort. Each destination offers unique experiences for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Trekking

Chembra Peak

Wayanad's highest point, ideal for trekking with a heart-shaped lake at the top. Experience panoramic views of the Western Ghats.

📍 8 km away
⏱️ 4-5 hours
Heritage

Edakkal Caves

Explore ancient rock carvings dating back to the Neolithic age. A fascinating journey through prehistoric art and history.

📍 12 km away
⏱️ 2-3 hours
Adventure

Soochipara Waterfalls

A stunning three-tiered waterfall perfect for adventure and photography. Enjoy rock climbing and natural pool swimming.

📍 15 km away
⏱️ 3-4 hours

⏱️ 2-3 hours
