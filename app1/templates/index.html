Our Advantages

Why Choose Us

Wake up to peaceful mornings surrounded by the lush greenery of Wayanad, where the calm rhythm of nature soothes your soul. Bali Bamboo Resort welcomes you with easy access from the main highway, ensuring a seamless travel experience. Each space is crafted with elegant bamboo design, offering a perfect fusion of natural aesthetics and modern-day comfort.

Savor a delightful variety of multi-cuisine dishes at our restaurant, ideal for family moments and joyful gatherings. With its close proximity to Wayanad’s top attractions—such as tea estates, waterfalls, and trekking routes—our resort is the perfect gateway to adventure and discovery.

Whether you’re looking for a quiet escape or an action-packed vacation, Bali Bamboo Resort offers the ideal blend of comfort, style, and convenience—making every stay a memorable one.
